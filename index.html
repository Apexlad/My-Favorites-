Reading


I love to read, I can and do read for hours a day. Just the thought that sitting on my couch and picking up a good book can be life-changing, it can transport you to a world of fantasy. A world where you can, well, do whatever you want to do. Personally my favorite book series is The Percy Jackson and the Olympians series. If you don't know what that is then: Percy Jackson is a Now York Times best selling series written by author Rick Riordan. The premise is that the ancient Greek Gods are still alive and kicking. Zues, Poseidon, Hades, you name it! The series is a penology ( 5 book series ) of The Lightning Thief, The Sea of Monsters, The Titans curse, The Battle of the labyrinth and finally the Last Olympian.
